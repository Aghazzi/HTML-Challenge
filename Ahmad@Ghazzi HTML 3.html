Although video game genres were once fairly clear cut, that's simply not the case these days. There's a growing variety of genres and sub-genres to understand, especially as game developers mix and blend different types of games in new and unexpected ways.
That means the video-gaming landscape is constantly evolving. Studios work on tight schedules and follow trends when the opportunity arises.
